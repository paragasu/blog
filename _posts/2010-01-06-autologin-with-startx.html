edit /etc/inittab
comment out the line

#1:2345:respawn:/sbin/getty 38400 tty1
add the line

1:2345:respawn:/bin/login -f YOUR_USER_NAME tty1 /dev/tty1 2>&1

edit the .bash_profile and add

startx
