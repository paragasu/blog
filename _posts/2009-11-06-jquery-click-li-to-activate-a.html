<ul class="menu">
<li><a href="/"> Home </a></li>
</ul>

to activate the link when user click on li

$('.menu li').click(function(e){
location.href = $(this).find('a').attr('href');
});

Now, user doesn't have to click on the "Home" to activate the link.
