Recently when i upgrade my debian linux. there is a very annoying problem on my iceweasel browser. all background image rendered ugly and whenever i scrolling the page, it leave a white space. It turn out, it is not iceweasel problem but Xorg and graphic card (i use intel GM965)

To fix, add in /etc/X11/xorg.conf on Device section

Option "AccelMethod" "XAA"
Option "XaaNoOffscreenPixmaps" "on"
